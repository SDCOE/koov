STEAM Academy

In partnership with Sony Electronics & the Classroom of the Future Foundation, the San Diego County Office of Education is excited to offer this FREE learning opportunity to all teachers throughout San Diego County.

WHO CAN PARTICIPATE?

The Sony KOOV STEAM Teacher Academy is FREE & open to all teachers throughout San Diego County through our partnership with Sony Electronics & the Classroom of the Future Foundation. We encourage educators who are considering robotics/coding in the Fall - virtually, blended, or in-person to sign up. 

SIGN UP NOW

WHAT IS KOOV?

All participants will receive a FREE KOOV trial kit to use in the classroom. KOOV is the all-in-one coding, robotics and design-kit that combines digital coding with physical building to teach the next generation of problem-solvers and innovators. To learn more, please visit Sony's KOOV Website

SIGN UP NOW

WHERE IS THIS BEING HELD?

All sessions will be held online through Sony's partnership with NYSCATE. During the one-day professional learning experience, attendees will participate in a virtual session and gain a working knowledge of robotics and coding. Additionally, participants will identify the possibilities and benefits of using Sony KOOV in their programs – both in classroom and virtually.

WHY IS THIS BEING OFFERED?

The San Diego County Office of Education (SDCOE) is dedicated to increasing youths’ experience with Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Math (STEAM). Educators can discover new ways to empower students to develop analytical thinking, creativity, collaboration, and problem-solving skills, while preparing them for the STEAM focused jobs of tomorrow.
